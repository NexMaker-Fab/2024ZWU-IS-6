How to keep safe when laser cutting?

1. All parts of the machine must be grounded completely reliably to prevent static electricity from injuring people.

2. During the operation of the machine, it is prohibited for operators to leave without authorization to avoid unnecessary losses.

3. During laser cutting and processing, the top cover plate of the machine must be covered to prevent the laser from deviating and injuring people.

4. Due to the presence of lasers and high-voltage components inside the machine, non professionals are strictly prohibited from dismantling the machine without authorization.

5. Due to the invisible nature of this type of laser, it is strictly prohibited to place flammable and explosive materials near the equipment to prevent laser misalignment and fire Disaster.

6. It is strictly prohibited to place any unrelated total reflection or diffuse reflection objects in the equipment to prevent direct laser reflection on the human body or On flammable materials.

7. During the operation of the machine, the operator must always observe the working condition of the machine (such as deformed edges, and the paper being laid) Whether the laser is blocked by the air pump blowing high, the machine makes abnormal noises, the temperature of the circulating water, etc.

8. Do not push or pull the trolley or crossbeam by hand.

9. The environment in which the machine is located is free from pollution, strong electricity, strong magnetism, and other interferences and impacts.

10. Do not turn on the machine when the voltage is unstable, otherwise a voltage regulator must be used.

11. Untrained personnel are prohibited from using.

12. The continuous working time of laser cutting processing shall not exceed 5 hours (with a break of at least half an hour during the process).

13. The water circulation must be kept clean, and the water temperature should not exceed 20-30 degrees (pure water is recommended).

14. It is strictly prohibited to turn on the ammeter at its maximum value to avoid breakdown of the laser power supply and shorten the lifespan of the laser tube.

15. The basic limitations of using laser power supply (i.e. the maximum current meter cannot exceed 20mA)

16. If the machine malfunctions or catches fire, please cut off the power immediately.

How to keep safe when using CNC?

1. Wear work clothes while working and do not allow wearing gloves to operate the machine tool.

2. Do not open the electrical protection door of the machine tool without permission, and do not make changes or delete system files inside the machine.

3. The workspace should be large enough.

4. When a certain task requires two or more people to work together, attention should be paid to mutual coordination and consistency.

5. Compressed air cleaning of machine tools, electrical cabinets, and NC units is not allowed.

6. Do not turn on the computer without the consent of the instructor.

7. Do not change the CNC system parameters or make any parameter settings.

8.Carefully check if the lubrication system is working properly. If the machine tool has not been started for a long time, it can be manually turned on first Each part is supplied with oil for lubrication.

9. The tools used should match the specifications allowed by the machine tool, and any severely damaged tools should be replaced in a timely manner.

10. Do not forget the tools used to adjust the cutting tools inside the machine tool.

11. After the installation of the cutting tool, the first and second trial cutting should be carried out.

12. Before processing, it is necessary to carefully check whether the machine tool meets the requirements, whether the tool is locked, and whether the workpiece is fixed Reliable. Run the verification program empty and check if the tool settings are correct.

13. Before starting the machine tool, the protective door of the machine tool must be closed.

14.Cannot come into contact with the rotating spindle or tool; When measuring workpieces, cleaning machines or equipment, please stop the machine first.

15. During the operation of the machine tool, the operator is not allowed to leave their position. If any abnormalities are found, the machine tool should be stopped immediately.

16. When there is a problem during processing, please press the reset button "RESET" to reset the system. In case of emergency, press the emergency stop button Press the button to stop the machine tool, but after returning to normal, make sure to reset each axis to the mechanical origin.

17. When manually changing the tool, be careful not to hit the workpiece or fixture. Attention should be paid when installing cutting tools on the turret of the machining center Whether the cutting tools interfere with each other.

18.Remove chips and wipe the machine tool to keep it and the environment clean.

19. Check the condition of lubricating oil and coolant, and add or replace them in a timely manner.

20. Turn off the power and main power on the machine tool control panel in sequence.

Learn different kind of material which can be manufacture by laser cutter

Metallic material

Carbon Steel : is one of the most common metalworking materials, widely used in construction, automotive, machinery manufacturing and other industries due to its strength and cost effectiveness. When laser cutting carbon steel, the appropriate auxiliary gas (oxygen or nitrogen) is selected according to the thickness, which can effectively control the cutting speed and edge oxidation.

Stainless Steel: Because of its corrosion resistance, it is widely used in kitchen equipment, medical equipment, decoration and other fields. When stainless steel is laser cut, the edge is almost free of oxidation, which is especially suitable for using nitrogen as an auxiliary gas to maintain the gloss and rust resistance of the material.

Aluminum alloy (Aluminum) : light weight and high strength, commonly found in aerospace, automotive, electronic products. When cutting aluminum alloys, due to its high reflectivity and thermal conductivity, it usually requires higher laser power and specialized cutting parameters, sometimes coupled with oxygen or nitrogen.

Copper and copper alloys (Copper & Brass) : Pure copper because of its high reflectivity and thermal conductivity, laser cutting is more difficult, but brass (a copper alloy) is relatively easy to cut, often used in ornaments, electronic parts and so on. When cutting copper, the choice of auxiliary gas is important to avoid overheating and material deformation.

Titanium and Titanium Alloys (Titanium & Titanium Alloys) : known for their high strength-to-weight ratio and good corrosion resistance, widely used in aerospace, biomedical fields. When laser cutting titanium alloy, due to its good thermal energy absorption, high-speed cutting can be achieved, but attention should be paid to controlling heat to avoid material property changes.

Non-metallic material

Acrylic (PMMA) : transparent, easy to process, often used in advertising, furniture decoration, lighting equipment and so on. Laser cut acrylics produce clean, smooth edges that do not require additional polishing.

Wood: Laser cut wood can create complex patterns and fine structures, suitable for crafts, model making, furniture decoration. When cutting wood, the laser can precisely control the degree of combustion to achieve different visual effects.

Paper & Cardboard: Laser cutting is widely used in the paper industry, such as greeting cards, packaging design, and model making. It allows for extremely high precision and complexity while keeping the edges clean.

Leather: Suitable for the fashion, accessories and homeware industries, laser-cut leather creates precise patterns and shapes while keeping the edges smooth and frayed.

Glass: Although laser cutting glass is a challenge because it is prone to cracking, modern technology has made it possible to achieve fine cutting and etching for art creation, architectural decoration, and more.

Special materials and others

Composite materials: such as carbon fiber reinforced plastics (CFRP), which are widely used in the aerospace and automotive industries, laser cutting can precisely separate the material without damaging its fiber structure.

Stone: Lasers can be used to precisely cut and carve stone for signs, monuments, artwork, etc., although the process can be slow and costly.
